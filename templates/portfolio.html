Information Warfare:

Russia's Use of Twitter during the 2016 US Presidential Election

PowerPoint Working Paper Code

On July 31, 2018, the website FiveThirtyEight published nearly 3 million tweets sent from Twitter handles connected to the Internet Research Agency(IRA), a Russian "troll factory" and a defendant in an indictment filed by the Justice Department in February 2018, as part of special counsel Robert Mueller's Russia investigation.

Campaign Finance Data:

An analysis of the 2016 US Presidential Election

This repository was created as part of a school project at the University of New Hampshire which asked students to create a Shiny app in R using a dataset of the students choice. I chose to work with campaign finance data for the 2016 election cycle, and I eventually settled on individual contributions to the the top 16 presidential contenders classified as transaction type 15, 15C, and 15E.

MN Crime Data

An exercise in data cleaning using R

Code

This project was completed as part of a school assignment for Prof. Phani Kidambi's Analytics Applications (DATA 900) course at the University of New Hampshire during the Fall 2018 semester. Students were given a dataset detailing various crimes committed in Minneapolis Minnesota during the years 2010 through 2016. The dataset was purposely altered by Prof. Kidambi to make it more challenging to clean.
